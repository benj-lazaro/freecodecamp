Christopher Pike

One of the Starfleet's finest captains in 2256

Here's the key dates of Captain Pike's life

  • Early 2200s: Born in Mojave, California on Earth
  • 2326: Graduates from Starfleet Academy
  • 2326-2250: Assigned to the USS Antares, USS Chatelet and then USS Aryabhatta
  • 2245-2250: Assigned to the USS Enterprise under Captain Robert April as First officer
  • 2250: Appointed as captain of the USS Enterprise
  • 2250s: Sets out on a Five-year mission
  • 2254: Achieved first contact with the Talosians
  • 2256-2257: Sits out the Federation-Klingon war
  • 2257-2258: Temporarily appointed captain of the USS Discovery and achieved first contact with the New Eden on Terralysium
  • 2258: Returns to the USS Enterprise
  • 2259: Achieved first contact with the Kiley on Kiley 279 and then the Shepherd
  • 2260s: Promoted to Fleet Captan
  • 2266: Exposed to delta radiation leaving him disfigured and confined to a wheelchair
  • 2267: With the assistance from the Talosians, is able to live out a life of illusion on Talos IV

"Chris, you set standards for yourself no one could meet. You treat everyone on board like a Human being except yourself."

-- Phil Boyce, 2254
